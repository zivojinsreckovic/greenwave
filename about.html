A Holistic Wellness Center

Green Wave Wellness is a place for people who want a clearer picture of their wellbeing—without pressure, without clinical jargon. For over two decades we’ve supported our community with calm, practical guidance.

We offer consultations and non-invasive body assessments as part of a broader wellness conversation. Our team supports you with personalized insight and practical guidance so you can make informed choices about your lifestyle and habits. No diagnosis, no treatment claims—just clarity and care.
